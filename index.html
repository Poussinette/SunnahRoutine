About This App

Sunnah Daily Coach guides you through the prophetic lifestyle of Muhammad ﷺ, based exclusively on authentic (sahih/hasan) narrations from the major hadith collections:

  • Sahih al-Bukhari — compiled by Imam Muhammad ibn Ismail al-Bukhari (d. 256 AH)
  • Sahih Muslim — compiled by Imam Muslim ibn al-Hajjaj (d. 261 AH)
  • Sunan Abi Dawud — compiled by Imam Abu Dawud al-Sijistani (d. 275 AH)
  • Jami' al-Tirmidhi — compiled by Imam al-Tirmidhi (d. 279 AH)
  • Sunan al-Nasa'i — compiled by Imam al-Nasa'i (d. 303 AH)
  • Sunan Ibn Majah — compiled by Imam Ibn Majah (d. 273 AH)
  • Musnad Ahmad — compiled by Imam Ahmad ibn Hanbal (d. 241 AH)
  • Riyad al-Salihin — compiled by Imam al-Nawawi (d. 676 AH)
  • Hisnul Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim) — compiled by Sa'id al-Qahtani

Each practice card displays its source reference so you can verify it yourself.
